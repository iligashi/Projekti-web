The Benefits of Using QR Codes on your Table Tents


Table tents, also known as table cards, are self-standing marketing materials created from printed and folded cardstock. Designed for tables, they help you upsell and cross-sell products and services for your culinary or retail business, like a cute version of the billboard.


1. Small space, more information

A QR Code provides instant access to a large amount of information hidden within its matrix. Table tents are a great opportunity to promote new drinks or menu items at restaurants for instance, or new products and services at your business convention booth. But you can only display so much text while still retaining aesthetic functionality in your design.

With QR Codes on table tents, you can now consolidate your menus into one, eye-catching table card. Help customers suffering from food allergies make informed choices by providing them with instant access to an online interactive menu with our Dynamic URL QR Code, which links to any landing page. By allowing your customers to customize their menu in accordance with their dietary or medical needs, you increase the likelihood of a positive experience in your eatery. When your customers have a positive experience, it translates to brand loyalty and can be the spark to word-of-mouth marketing. You can visit online resources like Menu Guide to get started, then use our QR Code to create a link from your table tent to your digital menu.


2. Bring the online food porn marketing to your restaurant


Provocative and glamorous photographs of food permeate social media. If you are in the food business, your dishes have probably been featured on someone’s Insta story and you are missing out, so take advantage of this free advertisement by linking customers directly to your social media accounts with our Social Media QR Code. Your contacts can go directly to each one of your profiles without having to type and search, and with a built-in Share button, they can forward your profile to their contacts and friends. Do not underestimate the power of food porn to get your customers hungry.


3. A mobile-friendly world offline

As one of the world's top QR Code users, China is already implementing QR Codes to create a wallet-free living and getting creative with how people interact with day to day life. Table tents are the perfect way to introduce your customers to your tech side. Does your business have a brand new app? Or maybe your eatery has an app that can streamline your customer's visit?

That's great, let your customers know by advertising it at your next event or at your restaurant with our App Store QR Code. FAQ

QR stands for “Quick Response.” It is a two-dimensional version of the barcode, granting instant access to hidden information within the Code once scanned. As an open-source technology with a broader information capacity, it is no wonder the QR Code is gaining in popularity. They can store up to 7089 digits or 4296 characters, including punctuation marks and special characters, with numbers and characters, as well as words and phrases (that is, internet addresses) having the ability to also be encoded.
